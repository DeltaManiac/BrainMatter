Symbolic Expression

Defined as 

1. an atom or
2. an expression of the form `(x.y)` where `x` and `y` are S-expressions

S-expressions can represent any [[algos.trees.binary]]


`(x y z)` => `(x.(y.(z.NIL)))` where NIL is the special end-of-list object
